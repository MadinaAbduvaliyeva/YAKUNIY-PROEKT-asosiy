We can't imagine our life without books. They play a very important part in our life. Books are our friends. They make our life more interesting. People read both for knowledge and for pleasure. Different people read different books. They help us with our lessons and work. We read serious books which help us understand the life, give us answers to questions which worry us, they make us think.
